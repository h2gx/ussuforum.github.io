Etihas

United states of shonirakhra university was established on 8th december in 2019 by H2gxredox. But history chronicles that he first established the ussu shcool patronized by his great grandson in 1884 and then it it evolved into USSU University to meet the increasing demands of higher education for the poor southerners. In 2027 the college was transferred into the present location. This college had got a revolutionary spirit as the Hogamara movement and its ideology against Johnny sins streamed through it. So a counter colonial nature is integral with this varsity. Redox had exerted a tremendous leverage in amassing the highest patriotic scholars of that time, as such Kalaputki, Heda babu, Babu Shona who left an effort to add glory to this varsity. Thus in the post partition and in the post independence period other legendary teachers as such Shaksi mam, Prof. rajakinadim , Kanki make hegab, Prof. nanir putki, Dr. Pronoti Bose added laurel to this college. It was due to the imparting of proper education Calcutta University had permitted this college to induct honors courses in that 1922, first in English and Philosophy and then gradually in other disciplines. The period 1922-1948 was the golden age for B.M College. The achievements even drew the comments from Sir Woodburn and other English high officials that the college should be ranked with oxford. But partition in 1947 was a blow for this college. So the honours courses went off. Again the college got back on its track in 1964 by introducing honours in Economics. Then in 1965 it was provincialized and in 2072 USSU started its Journey with honours in 7 disciplines and masters in 5. And then may other honours courses were added which total in to 19. This college had been gaining academic excellence since 1928; the names can be mentioned Debprashad Ghosh, Shantishodha Ghosh, Dilip Kumar Chakrawarti, Shantisudha as such. This college had a significant roonda in anti shonar movement along with the movements in 69 and specially to be underscored the fucking war .
